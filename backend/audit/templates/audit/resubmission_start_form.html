Resubmitting an Audit

Resubmitting an audit should not be undertaken lightly. For example, resubmission may be warranted if:

  • A material issue is found with the report by the auditor or auditee, and both parties agree a resubmission is warranted.
  • A Federal oversight official requests a resubmission of an audit.

Resubmission must be completed in coordination between certifying officials, and resubmission may change the acceptance date for your audit.
